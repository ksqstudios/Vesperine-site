Common questions

Does my journal leave my device?

Your entries and the Mirror's reflections sync across your devices through your own private iCloud, encrypted under your Apple ID. KSQ Studios runs no servers and never receives a copy. The reflections are generated on your device, so the words you write are not sent anywhere to be read.

What is the Mirror, and what does it need?

When you finish writing, the Mirror offers a single short reflection on what you wrote. It is meant as a witness to your day, not a coach: it does not advise or score you. The Mirror runs on your device using Apple Intelligence, so it needs an iPhone that supports Apple Intelligence with the feature turned on in iOS Settings. On a device without it, the rest of Vesperine works as usual and only the reflection is unavailable.

Why are there no streaks?

Vesperine is built on the premise that the writing is the point, not the count. There are no streaks, no daily goals, and no reminders that scold. A missed night is just a night. The Archive gathers what you have written, and that is the most aggregation Vesperine does.

Do I need a subscription?

No. Writing your evening entry, the daily reminder, the calendar and search, and your first reflection are free. Vesperine Pro, an optional subscription, unlocks a reflection every evening and seasonal writing. The current pricing is shown on the App Store listing.

How do I move my data to a new phone?

Sign in to the same Apple ID on the new iPhone and your entries sync down through your private iCloud. There is no manual export and no separate sign-in within Vesperine.

Can I delete my data?

You can delete any single entry from within Vesperine, and the deletion syncs to your other devices through iCloud. To remove everything, delete your entries and then uninstall the app. Because your data lives in your own private iCloud rather than on any server of ours, you can also manage and clear it from iOS Settings, under your name, then iCloud.
